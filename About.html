Kara Thrace

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 25 years of experience in deep-space logistics, starship engineering, and orbital fleet operations, Kara Thrace founded SPEAR with a singular belief: to redefine the standards of spacecraft manufacturing. A respected engineer known for her precision, calm leadership, and relentless pursuit of excellence, Kara spent decades working on high-profile shipbuilding projects across the inner systems. Her expertise in structural refinement and propulsion efficiency became the backbone of SPEAR’s manufacturing philosophy. Under her guidance, SPEAR has grown from a small orbital workshop into one of the most trusted names in starship manufacturing, known for engineering ships that are reliable, elegant, and built for the long haul.

Our Story

SPEAR began as a small orbital engineering team working within a modest shipyard suspended above Ganymede. In those early years, Kara Thrace and her crew focused on crafting personal and mid-size ships that prioritized reliability and pilot comfort. While other manufacturers chased mass production, SPEAR invested in refinement: stronger hulls, smarter systems, more customizable interiors, and intuitive navigation technologies. Word spread quickly. Pilots trusted our ships not because they were flashy, but because they were dependable. Over time, SPEAR expanded into a sprawling orbital shipyard complex with the capability to design, assemble, and test a full range of vessels—from personal transports to advanced long-range cruisers. Every ship that leaves our platforms still carries that same core promise: built with care, tested with integrity, and refined for the journey ahead.

Our Mission

At SPEAR, our mission is to engineer spacecraft that embody reliability, innovation, and adaptability, vessels crafted to endure the unknown and thrive in the vastness of space. We are committed to precision manufacturing, rigorous refinement, and pushing the boundaries of aerospace technology, all while maintaining an unwavering focus on quality and pilot-centric design. Our goal is to empower explorers, traders, and adventurers with ships that not only meet but exceed the demands of their journeys, delivering safety, performance, and confidence in every voyage. Through meticulous craftsmanship and forward-driven engineering, we build starships that inspire bold missions and enable humanity to reach beyond the horizon.
